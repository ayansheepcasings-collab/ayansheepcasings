Welcome to Ayan Ali Sheep Casing

Ayan Ali Sheep Casing is a trusted name in the global natural casings export industry. We excel in processing high-quality natural sheep, goat, and beef casings under strict international hygiene protocols. Every casing is carefully selected, measure-calibrated, salted, and tubed to meet the exacting standards of our buyers worldwide.

Our Premium Natural Casings

We export high-grade natural casings sourced strictly from Sheep, Goat & Beef.

Sheep Casings

Highly elastic and tender natural sheep casings, perfect for fresh, cooked, and dry sausages.

  • Calibers: 14/16, 16/18, 18/20, 20/22, 22/24, 24/26 mm
  • Quality Grades: Grade AA, AB, BC
  • Packaging: Hard Tubes, Soft Tubes & Salted Hanks

Goat Casings

Renowned for exceptional wall strength and uniform diameter, suitable for high-speed filling.

  • Calibers: 14/16, 16/18, 18/20, 20/22, 22/24, 24/26 mm
  • Strength: High Burst Pressure Resistance
  • Packaging: Vacuum Sealed Barrels & Salted Rings

Beef Casings

Heavy-duty natural beef casings (Rounds, Middles, and Bungs) for thick, cured sausages and salamis.

  • Types: Beef Rounds All Calibers (38/43 mm), Middles
  • Usage: Salami, Bologna, Cooked Sausages
  • Packaging: Export Standard Casks & Cans
